About Me

Hi, I'm Tim!

I am a researcher in the field of natural language processing and knowledge engineering.

Currently, I am a researcher at ScaDS.AI and TU Dresden. I work on different projects focusing on natural language processing, language models, and knowledge graphs. Additionally, I enjoy working on open source projects and writing AI-related articles on Medium.

During my university studies I put my major focus on Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Data Science. The participation in a lecture internal Kaggle competition and the Data Mining Cup have aroused my curiosity in this area. In addition to deepening my theoretical knowledge, I worked as Data Science Intern in the Aftersales department at Mercedes-Benz Group. During my master's thesis I researched defenses against adversarial attacks on deep neural networks for autonomous driving at MHP - A Porsche Company.

Through continuous improvement and growth, I strive to develop impactful AI solutions that simplify lives and address high-impact challenges.
